UGotGDM

Gestational Diabetes Tracking Made Simple

An iOS app for tracking gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM) glucose readings and food intake.

Features

  • Daily glucose monitoring at four key times (fasting and post-meal)
  • Food diary with meal and snack tracking
  • Color-coded visual feedback (green = in target, red = out of range)
  • Historical data view with trends
  • Export to text, CSV, and PDF formats
  • Customizable notifications and reminders
  • Premium features: Interactive charts and advanced reports

Tech Stack

SwiftUI CoreData StoreKit

Overview

UGotGDM helps you monitor your daily glucose levels and record food intake during pregnancy. The app provides visual feedback on whether readings are within target ranges and lets you export data for sharing with healthcare providers.

Privacy First

All your health data stays completely private on your device using CoreData. No cloud sync means your glucose readings and food entries never leave your phone.
