UGotGDM

Gestational Diabetes Tracking Made Simple

UGotGDM is an iOS app designed to help you track glucose readings and food intake during pregnancy. With a clean interface and powerful features, managing gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM) has never been easier.

Key Features

Today View

See your day at a glance with four daily glucose readings (fasting, breakfast, lunch, dinner) and a complete food diary. Color-coded visual feedback shows whether your readings are in target range (green) or need attention (red).

History View

Review past days with a scrollable calendar. Track your progress over time and identify patterns in your glucose levels and diet.

Charts & Analytics

Premium feature: View interactive charts showing your glucose trends over time. Identify which meals or foods might be affecting your readings.

Export Your Data

Share your health data with your healthcare provider in multiple formats: plain text, CSV for spreadsheets, or professionally formatted PDF reports. All exports include both glucose readings and food entries for comprehensive tracking.

Reminders & Notifications

Set customizable reminders for glucose testing times and meal logging. Never miss a reading with gentle notifications throughout the day.

Privacy First

All your health data stays completely private on your device using CoreData. No cloud sync means your sensitive glucose readings and food entries never leave your phone.

Who Is This For?

Pregnant individuals who have been diagnosed with gestational diabetes and need to track glucose levels daily.

Healthcare providers who want their patients to have an easy way to share comprehensive glucose and food logs.

Anyone who values privacy and wants health data to stay exclusively on their device.

Why UGotGDM?

Built with input from individuals who have managed GDM. UGotGDM focuses on the essential features that matter most: easy tracking, clear visual feedback, and simple data export for your healthcare team. No unnecessary complexity, just the tools you need.
