Ill Technique

Step in the cypher

A utility for freestyle emcees to practice and improve their skills.

Features

  • 4 unique modes (Practice, Cypher, Battle, Freestyle)
  • Adjustable tempo
  • Library of instrumental hip-hop tracks
  • Audio engine that automatically mixes tracks
  • Collection of 4000+ words to inspire your flow

Tech Stack

Foundation UIKit AVKit StoreKit

Overview

Ill Technique is the ultimate utility for freestyle emcees. Whether you're practicing your flow, warming up before a battle, or just having fun with wordplay, Ill Technique provides the tools you need to elevate your freestyle game.
