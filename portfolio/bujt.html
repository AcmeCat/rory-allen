Bujt

Manage your money

A simple, no-nonsense budgeting app designed to help you track your spending.

Features

  • Weekly budget snapshot - see remaining balance at a glance
  • Track expenses by category
  • Quick input flow for adding expenses
  • Flexible category management
  • All data stays private on your device

Tech Stack

Foundation SwiftUI CoreData

Overview

Bujt is a simple, no-nonsense budgeting app designed to help you track your spending without the complexity of traditional finance apps. See where your money goes, set limits, and make informed spending decisions—all in one streamlined view.

Privacy First

All your financial data stays completely private on your device using CoreData. No cloud sync means complete privacy—your spending habits are yours alone.

Availability

Coming soon to the iOS App Store.
